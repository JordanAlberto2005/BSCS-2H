MY SKILLS

CRITICAL THINKING

-Critical thinking involves analyzing facts to form a judgment. It is crucial for problem-solving, decision-making, and evaluating the credibility of information. With the vast amount of data and opinions available, especially online, the ability to think critically helps individuals distinguish between reliable information and misinformation. It also enables more effective and logical problem-solving in personal and professional contexts.

EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE

-Emotional Intelligence is the ability to recognize, understand, manage, and reason with emotions, both in oneself and others. It is important for leadership, teamwork, and communication. People with high EQ can better manage stress, navigate social complexities, and make more thoughtful decisions.They also tend to have stronger relationships and can influence and inspire others effectively.

ADAPTABILITY

-Adaptability is the ability to adjust to new conditions or changes in the environment. This skill is increasingly important in a world that is rapidly changing due to technological advancements, economic shifts, and global events. Being adaptable allows individuals and organizations to pivot strategies, learn new skills, and stay competitive. It involves being open to change, willing to learn continuously, and being resilient in the face of challenges.
